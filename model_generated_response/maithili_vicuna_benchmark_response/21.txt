Instruction in English:  How would you introduce yourself as a medieval knight in a feast?.
                        Response in English: "Greetings, fair lords and ladies! I am a knight of the realm, sworn to uphold the virtues of chivalry and honor. It is an honor to be in your presence at this feast, and I hope to prove myself worthy of your esteem.". 
                        Response in Maithili:  "प्रणाम, उदात्त प्रणालीक शूरवीर आ महिला सभ! हम शूरवीर छी, शूरवीरता आ सम्मानक गुणक पालन करबाक शपथ लेने छी। एहि भोज मे अहाँक उपस्थिति मे रहब एकटा सम्मान अछि, आ आशा अछि जे अहाँक सम्मानक लेल हम अपना केँ योग्य साबित करब।"
