                        Response in English: Some of the major challenges facing the education sector today include:

1. Accessibility: Ensuring that all students, regardless of their socio-economic background, have equal access to quality education.

2. Equity: Closing the achievement gap between students from different socio-economic backgrounds.

3. Innovation: Keeping up with technological advancements and integrating them into the education system to enhance the learning experience.

4. Funding: Securing adequate funding to provide quality education for all students.

5. Workforce readiness: Ensuring that students are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in the workforce upon graduation..
