Instruction in English:  How can governments use fiscal and monetary policies to deal with an economic downturn? Your response must be less than 6 sentences..
                        Response in English: Governments can use fiscal and monetary policies to deal with an economic downturn. Fiscal policy involves the use of government spending, taxation, and borrowing to influence the economy. During an economic downturn, governments can increase spending on infrastructure, social programs, and tax cuts to stimulate demand and create jobs. Monetary policy, on the other hand, involves actions taken by the central bank to influence the money supply and interest rates. During an economic downturn, the central bank can lower interest rates to encourage borrowing and spending, and can also purchase government bonds to increase the money supply and stimulate the economy..
