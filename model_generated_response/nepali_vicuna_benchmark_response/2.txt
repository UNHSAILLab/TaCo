Instruction in English:  What are the most effective ways to cope with stress?.
                        Response in English: The most effective ways to cope with stress include:

1. Practicing relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.
2. Engaging in physical activity such as walking, running, or swimming.
3. Connecting with supportive friends or family members.
4. Getting enough sleep and rest.
5. Eating a healthy diet and avoiding caffeine, alcohol, and other stimulants.
6. Practicing mindfulness and focusing on the present moment..
