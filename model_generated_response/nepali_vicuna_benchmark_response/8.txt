Instruction in English:  What are some of the major challenges in the education sector today?.
                        Response in English: Some of the major challenges in the education sector today include:

1. Access to quality education: Many children, especially in developing countries, do not have access to quality education. This is due to factors such as poverty, geographical location, and lack of infrastructure.

2. Teacher shortage: There is a global shortage of qualified teachers, especially in developing countries. This can lead to overcrowded classrooms and a lack of individual attention for students.

3. Outdated curriculum: The curriculum in many schools is outdated and does not adequately prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century. This can lead to a lack of critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

4. High dropout rates: Many students, especially in low-income areas, drop out of school before completing their education. This is due to factors such as poverty, lack of parental support, and a lack of relevance in the curriculum.

5. Lack of funding: Education is often underfunded, especially in developing countries. This can lead to a lack of resources such as textbooks, technology, and qualified teachers..
