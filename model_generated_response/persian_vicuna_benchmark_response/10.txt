                        Response in English: The most effective strategies for resolving conflict in the workplace include:

1. Active listening: Encouraging all parties involved in the conflict to listen to each other's perspectives without interrupting or judging.

2. Open communication: Encouraging open and honest communication between all parties involved in the conflict.

3. Empathy: Showing empathy and understanding towards all parties involved in the conflict.

4. Collaborative problem-solving: Encouraging all parties to work together to find a mutually beneficial solution to the conflict.

5. Conflict resolution training: Providing conflict resolution training to employees to help them learn effective strategies for resolving workplace conflicts.

6. Mediation: Involving a neutral third party to help facilitate the resolution of the conflict..
