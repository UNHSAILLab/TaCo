This allows quantum computers to share information quickly and efficiently. The third principle is quantum tunneling, where particles can pass through barriers that would normally be impenetrable. This allows quantum computers to perform operations that are difficult or impossible for classical computers. By combining these principles, quantum computers can solve complex problems much faster than classical computers..
